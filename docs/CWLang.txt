SYNTAX:
- DURATION_TRIGGER(CONDITION):EFFECT(TARGET(), PARAM)
- TARGETS: CHOICE(<target>), RANDOM(<targets>), FILTER(<condition>)
- TRIGGER:SEMICOLON-SEPARATED EFFECT(S)

TRIGGERS:
- START OF TURN (SOT):
    SOT, SOT(<condition>)
- WHILE IN PLAY (WIP):
    WIP, maybe WIP(<condition>)
- COST (CST):
    CST([<effect>]) // e.g. FLOOP(SELF), DISCARD(PLAYER, 1)
- SELF ENTERS PLAY (SEP):
    SEP, SEP(<condition>)
- SELF EXITS PLAY (SXP):
    SXP, SXP(<condition>)
- END OF TURN (EOT):
    EOT, maybe EOT(<condition>)
- WHEN SELF ATTACKS (WSA):
    WSA, WSA(<target>)

- NO_EFFECT
- UNTIL_EOT(<effect>)
- FOREACH([<target>], <effect>]
- DRAW(<target>, int)
- SHUFFLE(<target>)
- SEARCH(<target>)
- TO_HAND(<target>, <target>, int) // target player, target card
- DISCARD(<target>, int)
- DESTROY(<target>)
- EXILE(<target>)
- FIGHT(<target>, <target>)
- FLOOP(<target>)
- SET_INVULN(<target>, <bool>)
- SET_UNTARG(<target>, <bool>)
- HEAL(<target>, <int>)
- DEAL_DMG(<target>, <int>)
- ATTACK(<target>, <target>)
- MOD_ATK(<target>, <int>)
- MOD_DEF(<target>, <int>)
- SELECT([<target>], <range>)
- CHOOSE([<target>], [<options>])
- SET_TARGET(<target>)
- MOVE ...
- SWAP_LANES ...
